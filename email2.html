Welcome to Think College Conversations!

Think College Conversations is an online tool to gather and offer support and information.

Getting Started:

  • Look for your email from Slack inviting you to join Think College Conversations.
  • Follow the steps to sign up.
  • Click on Explore Slack.
  • New users will get a brief tour of Slack from a Slackbot (not as dangerous as it sounds!!) explaining what channels are and how to navigate them, how to find others in the group, and how to invite new users to join the TC Conversations.
